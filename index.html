Welcome to Sliding Puzzle!

Test your puzzle-solving skills in this classic sliding game! Your goal is to arrange the numbered squares in ascending order by sliding them into the blank space.

All the best!

Mouse/Touch Controls

  • Click a Square: Click any square in the same row or column as the blank square to move it toward the blank square.
  • Multiple Squares: Clicking a square may move multiple squares at once if they are in the same row or column as the blank square.

Keyboard Controls

  • Arrow Keys: Use the arrow keys to move the blank square in the desired direction.
  • Shift + Arrow Keys: Hold Shift and press arrow key to move the blank square to the edge of the grid.
